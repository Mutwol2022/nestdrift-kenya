# NestDrift Kenya — Monetisation Strategy

**Version 2.0 | April 2026**

---

## Overview

NestDrift Kenya is a two-sided rental marketplace connecting landlords with verified tenants across all 47 Kenyan counties. This document outlines six revenue streams adapted for the Kenyan market.

**Projected monthly revenue (early stage): KES 550,000 – 1,620,000**

---

## Revenue Stream Summary

| Stream | Primary Payer | Est. Monthly (Early Stage) |
|--------|--------------|---------------------------|
| Featured Listings | Landlords | KES 150,000 – 400,000 |
| Lead Generation Fees | Landlords | KES 80,000 – 250,000 |
| Agency Subscriptions | Agencies | KES 200,000 – 600,000 |
| Tenant Services | Tenants | KES 50,000 – 150,000 |
| Affiliate Commissions | Partners | KES 40,000 – 120,000 |
| Data Monetisation | B2B | KES 30,000 – 100,000 |

## 2. Lead Generation Fees

Charged to landlords when tenants take verified actions.

| Tenant Action | Fee |
|--------------|-----|
| Taps WhatsApp | KES 150 |
| Books a tour | KES 300 |
| Submits application | KES 500 |
| Confirmed lease referral | 50% of 1st month's rent |
